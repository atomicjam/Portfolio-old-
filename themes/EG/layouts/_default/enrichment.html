Enrichment

enrichment.cheltladiescollege.org

Organisation of Wellbeing & Enrichment activities for the school term requires large amounts of administration time to ensuring each activity is staffed, located, provisioned correctly to alow the events to run smoothly.

Enrichment was built to streamline the process, allowing management of events and activities & automatically flagging issues around clashes, or missing resources which can then be addressed by the administrator.

Initial built by a member of staff using Excel, the system took the underpinning process and reimagined it using the Laravel Framework.

This allowed Students & Staff can login and see details of the activities, making internal communication around events significantly easier. Users can subscribe to have the events added to their Outlook calendar.

Upcoming activity details are shown on the College digital displays for those who have not subscribed.

The system generates reports for the various internal departments providing details in advance to those administrators that require access.

Administrators are guided through adding activities to events, and the system will prompt if clashes are found, or a member of staff has been over provisioned.
