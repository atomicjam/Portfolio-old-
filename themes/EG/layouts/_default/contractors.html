Contractors

contactors.cheltladiescollege.org

Contractors system provides a centralised system to store the compliance data around each of the contractors The Cheltenham Ladies' College uses.

Each contractor is presented with a portal they can complete the required documents, documents can be check by the CLC sponsor for compliance.

The system automaticlly chases expired paperwork and provides reports to Health & Safety and Compliance teams to ensure DBS checks, Risk assessments e.t.c are in place before work begins.
